Lionel Messi - The Greatest Football Player Today

(Of Course Not Better Than Pele)

Quick Facts

  • He is a captain in the national soccer team of Argentina.
  • He plays as a right forward on FC Barcelona.
  • Messi is the first player to win the European Golden Shoes for three times
  • Lionel holds a record of 82 goals scored in only one season of La Liga
  • And, finally, Messi was born with a growth hormone deficiency as a child, but still came out to become one of the most successful football players in history

Lionel has won many great awards throughout his career of playing football including:

  1. World Soccer player of the year
  2. FIFA player of the year
  3. European Golden Shoe
  4. And, the UEFA best player in Europe
