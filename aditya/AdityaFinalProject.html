The Arenas

The First Arena

This is the training camp, where you learn to play! It consists of 7 battles, and 12 different cards. You do not gain or lose any trophies in this arena, but you get new cards, along with one guaranteed card of the rarity epic.

The cards of rarity common in the training camp are the following

The Knight

Archers

Bomber

Arrows

The cards of rarity rare in the training camp are the following

Fireball

Mini Pekka

Musketeer

Giant

The cards of rarity epic in the training camp are the following

The Prince

Baby Dragon

Skeleton Army

Witch
