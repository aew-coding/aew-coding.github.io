Avoid Stranger Danger

Start by entering a phone number. Before you do business, meet or interact with anyone, check what others have experienced by simply entering their phone number. We'll return a count of positive and negative interactions from users such as yourself so you can judge for yourself.

Had a good experience? There's nothing more to do. Had a bad experience? Return to this site in as little as an hour after your initial search and submit a negative mark. No elaborate explanations or justifications, just let the numbers speak for themselves.

Love the site? Spread the word on Twitter and Facebook!

All reputation checks and negative marks are absolutely anonymous. No one will ever know who checked their reputation online or left a negative mark, and negative marks cannot be erased so you can be confidant in the results you see.

International Support

You can enter phone numbers in any international format. Simply select the country and we convert the number to E164 standard and return results.
