    • How To Make Your Own Plant Pot



    1. Fill a bowl with water


    2. Swirl different colors of paint in the water and then stir lightly until it looks like swirls.


    3. Dip a white flower pot into the mixture until it looks how you want it to.


    4. Let it dry.


    5. Fill with soil and plant your seed or put a plant in. Make sure to water!
