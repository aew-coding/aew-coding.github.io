Mope.io Animals







There are many animals in the game, Mope.io. As you play the game, your animal will level up depending on its amount of xp.






Land Animals





Mouse


The mouse is the most basic land animal in mope.io. It can eat red berries and can't eat any other animals. A mouse has no abilities. A mouse has zero to fourty-nine xp.


Rabbit


The rabbit is the second land animal in mope.io. It can eat red berries and plankton. The rabbit can eat other players too including the mouse, shrimp, and chipmunk. The rabbit has no abilities. A rabbit has fifty to one hundred and fourty-nine xp.


Pig


The pig is the third land animal in mope.io. It can eat red berries, small mushrooms, and plankton. The pig can eat other players too including the mouse, shrimp, chipmunk, rabbit, trout, and arctic hare. A pig can move quickly in mud A rabbit has one hundred and fifty to one hundred and two hundred and ninety-nine xp.


And there are many more animals in mope.io!!!
